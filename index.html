Welcome to the clinic of Dr. Ari Abramson - where your smile has been at the top of our priorities for 35 years.

Comprehensive dental care can be unnerving, and it is for this reason that many people avoid it, and continue to experience pain, or struggle with aesthetic issues. That’s why it is so important to choose a dental professional who is attentive to your needs and puts your satisfaction above all else.

Dr. Abramson’s clinic specializes in the following treatments:

  • 1Smile design and facial aesthetics
  • 2Full or partial oral rehabilitation
  • 3General dentistry

Dr. Ari Abramson is a second generation dentist. He is a graduate of Hebrew University at Hadassa Ein Karem, and a member of the prestigious international Spear Education Advanced Studies in oral rehabilitation.

Our clinic is located in the heart of Jerusalem, and offers diagnosis and treatment with full patient involvment, in a pleasant and laid-back atmosphere.

Smile Design

Sometimes a smile is worth a thousand words.

Smile design is the delicate art of planning and improving the appearance of the teeth with minimal modification to the structure of the tooth. With the help of advanced, minimally invasive treatments, you can have a brand new smile.
