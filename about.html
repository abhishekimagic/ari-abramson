About Us

WE CARE ABOUT YOUR SMILE

Welcome to the clinic of Dr. Ari Abramson - where your smile has been at the top of our priorities for 35 years.

Comprehensive dental care can be unnerving, and it is for this reason that many people avoid it, and continue to experience pain, or struggle with aesthetic issues. That’s why it is so important to choose a dental professional who is attentive to your needs and puts your satisfaction above all else.

Dr. Abramson’s approach is both innovative and conservative. When it comes to decisions about your dental health, every tooth gets individualized attention, and all treatment plans are customized to your needs.

We invite you to make an appointment with Dr. Abramson today and start enjoying a noticeable improvement in your quality of life.
